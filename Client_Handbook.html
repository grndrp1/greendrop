Managing Your New Site

PART 01

Accessing the Site Editor

1

Log In to Sanity Studio

Go to your custom editor URL (usually https://greendrop-web.sanity.studio). Log in using your Google or Sanity credentials. This is where 100% of your website content lives.

● Pro Tip for New Users

The left-hand sidebar contains all your Documents. Think of "Documents" as the master records for your Locations, Services, and Pages.

PART 02

The Page Builder

1

Find Your Page

Select "Pages" from the sidebar. Choose the page you want to edit (e.g., Home, About Us, Loaner Cars).

2

Adding & Customizing Sections

Most pages use a "Page Builder." You can add new sections like Hero Banners, Reviews, or Text Blocks. You can drag and drop sections to rearrange them on the page instantly.

PART 03

Managing HTML Form Embeds

interactive elements like the Contact Form, Loaner Car Form, and Reviews Widgets are powered by external partners like Cognito Forms and Elfsight. You update them using the "HTML Embed" block.

1

Copy Your Embed Code

Go to your partner dashboard (e.g., Cognito Forms). Copy the <script> or <iframe> code snippet they provide for your form.

2

Insert the Block in Sanity

In Sanity, open the page where you want the form. Click "Add item" in the Page Builder and select "HTML Embed / Form".

3

Paste and Publish

Paste the code snippet into the "HTML Embed Code" field. Give it an internal label (like "Main Contact Form") so you can find it later. Click the green Publish button.

⚡ Specific Form Instructions

Contact Form: To update the main contact form, look for the "Contact Us" page document. The form is usually a block near the bottom of the section list.

Loaner Car Form: To update the Loaner Cars page, find the "Loaner Cars" page and look for the block labeled Raw HTML or Form Embed.

PART 04

Neighborhood Garage Management

Managing your 6 locations is easy. When you update a Location document, it automatically updates the map, the footer, and the dedicated location sub-pages.

1

Phone, Hours, and Map

Inside a Location document, you can update the phone number and business hours. For the Map, you simply update the Latitude and Longitude fields to move the shop's pin on the map.

2

Service Exceptions

If a specific shop doesn't perform Brakes (rare!), you can edit that specifically in the "Service Location" documents to hide it from that shop's menu.

PART 06

Infrastructure & Hosting

Your website is powered by an "Enterprise-Grade" modern stack. Instead of one heavy server, your site is distributed across three world-class platforms for maximum speed and security.

G

GitHub: The Source Archive

Think of GitHub as your website's safe. It stores every single piece of code and maintains a full history of every change ever made.

Open GitHub Repository →

V

Vercel: The Engine

Vercel is your host. It makes sure the site loads instantly and manages your security (SSL) and domain names.

Open Vercel Dashboard →

S

Sanity: The Database

Sanity stores your data (images, text, prices). This is the "brain" behind the content you edit.

Open Sanity Studio →

🚀 How it all connects

When you click Publish in Sanity, it sends a signal to Vercel. Vercel then grabs the updated data and "re-packages" your website instantly. This is "Serverless" technology—no maintenance or patching required on your part!

Final Launch Tips

  • Always click Publish — changes don't go live until you do!
  • Use Ctrl+S (or Cmd+S) to quickly save drafts.
  • If an image looks blurry, ensure you are uploading a high-resolution JPG or PNG (aim for 2000px wide for banners).
  • If the "Restoration" of a page looks wrong, check that the Slug (the URL part) hasn't accidentally changed.
